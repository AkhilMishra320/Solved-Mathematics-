Equations Reducible To Linear Form : Bernoulli's Equation Questions

An equation of the form dy/dx + Py = Qy^n

Multiplying throughout by y^-n, the equation becomes

y^-n dy/dx + P y^ (-n+1) = Q

Now put y^(-n+1) = v so that (-n+1) y^-n dy/dx = dv/dx

dv/dx (1-n) Pv = (1-n) Q

I.F = e^∫(1-n)pdx

The general solution of equation

y (I.F) = ∫(1-n)Q (I.F)dx + c

2.)When m1, m2, m3 are same

C.F = (c1 + c2x +c3x²) e^mx

3.)Whem m = a + Bi and m = a - Bi

C.F = e^ax ( c1 cosBx + c2 sinBx)

4.)Whem m = a + √B and m = a - √B

C.F = e^ax ( c1 cosh√Bx + c2 sinh√Bx)
